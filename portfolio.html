portfolio

Oshi-Gokko

Original arcade game. I was primarily in charge of the cabinet's design and assembly. Collaboration with Kitty Guo, Mashi Zaman, An-Kai Cheng.


For Amusement Only

VR storytelling project crossed with a custom-built pinball controller and a mock neural interface wristband.


There Is a Hole

Minimalist Bitsy game, available to play at my itch.io page.


Audio Reactive Gundam Helmet (RX-69)

Wearable 3D printed helmet for a performance at ITP and Friends. Collaboration with Jess Shen on the lights and programming.


Crab Mouse

Custom hardware made during the 2024 Stupid Hackathon. A mouse designed for letting humans surf the web like crabs (you pinch both buttons to click, it moves easier side-to-side). A companion piece to Brent Bailey's Crabify browser extension.


K5 Robot Visual Journalism Piece

Researched and wrote an article on the K5 robot used by the NYPD in Fall 2023. Also sculpted and textured the piece's 3D model of the robot. Collaboration with Jane Cheng and Athena Zhou.


Playces

My ITP thesis project. A three-part collection exploring level design and the sense of place created by famous maps from '90s and 2000's FPS games.


Reptilia PCB

Project for a class called Homemade Hardware. Custom PCB milled using an Othermill CNC. Contains circuitry for two distortional pedals for changing the output sound of an Arduino Uno.


CARPG

Created low-poly, low-resolution 3D assets for this prototype RPG game combat system. Video forthcoming. Collaboration with Mashi Zaman.
