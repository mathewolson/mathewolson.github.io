BIO

I'm a writer. I focus on topics in tech and entertainment. As of August 2019, I work as a reporter at USgamer.net.

Previously, I worked as an associate editor at Digg, where I helped curate articles and videos for the front page while also working on original pieces for the site. I've also done freelance writing and research for The Wirecutter.

From 2015 to 2017, my friend Ashley and I worked on a Twin Peaks fan podcast.

I studied Economics at Reed College. While at Reed I also studied non-fiction writing, 20th century literature, environmental policy, and statistics.

I'm from Kirkland, WA (proud home of Costco).
