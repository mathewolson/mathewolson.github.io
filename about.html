ABOUT

Mathew Olson is a graduate of Reed College. He is the 2015 recipient of the Gerald M. Meier Award for Distinction in Economics.

Mat has cultivated skills in audio, video, and print production. He is the co-host of Can You Get To That? and Twin Peaks Peeks. He is also the creator of Go To Bed with Mat Olson, a late night talk show. In 2014, Mat was an editor and the layout designer for Reed's 2014 Student Body Handbook. A sample of that work can be seen here.

While at Reed, Mat authored a couple blog posts for the alumni magazine, about alumni giving and a beloved staff member.
