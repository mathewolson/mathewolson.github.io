BIO

Mathew Olson is a graduate of Reed College. He is the 2015 recipient of the Gerald M. Meier Award for Distinction in Economics. Some data visualization examples can be seen here, and questions about STATA, R, and qGIS are welcome.

Mat's formal studies include economics, statistics, public policy, literature, environmental politics, familial histories, computing theory, and personal essay writing. Other pursuits outside of the classroom include screenprinting, calligraphy, event planning, and improvisational theatre.

Mat has cultivated skills in digital, audio/video, and print production. He is the co-host of the podcasts Can You Get To That? and Twin Peaks Peeks. He is also the creator of Go To Bed with Mat Olson, a late night talk show. In 2014, Mat was an editor and the layout designer for that year's Reed Student Body Handbook. A digital sample of that work can be seen here.

Stemming from the handbook, Mat wrote posts for Reed's alumni magazine about alumni giving and a beloved staff member.

Mat knows that both his first and last name are easy to misspell. It just happens.
