BIO

I'm a writer focused on topics in tech, gaming, and entertainment. I'm launching a new AR/VR newslettter for The Information in March 2021.

Previously, I worked as a reporter at USgamer.net. You can find links to all of that work—daily news posts, features, reviews, op-eds—by clicking right here. Before that, I was an associate editor at Digg, where I helped curate articles and videos for the front page while also working on original pieces for the site. I've also done freelance writing and research for Wirecutter.

From 2015 to 2017, my friend Ashley and I worked on a podcast about Twin Peaks.

I studied Economics at Reed College. While at Reed I also studied non-fiction writing, 20th century literature, environmental policy, and statistics.

I'm from Kirkland, WA (proud home of Costco).
