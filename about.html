BIO

I'm a writer. I focus on topics in tech and entertainment, with some natural overlap in labor, politics and policy. As of July 2017, I work as an associate editor at Digg.

I've co-hosted and edited three wildly different podcasts: Twin Peaks Peeks, Can You Get To That? and Finite Jest.

I studied Economics at Reed College. While at Reed I also studied non-fiction writing, 20th century literature, environmental policy and statistics.

I'm from Kirkland, WA (proud home of Costco).
